What's Inside

1

Pre-Algebra Review

Numbers and their properties, order of operations, fractions, variables and expressions — the vocabulary the rest of the book assumes.

2

The Properties of Equality

Reflexive, symmetric, transitive, substitution, addition, multiplication, commutative, associative, and distributive properties — every step in algebra, named and justified.

3

Signed Numbers

The number line, absolute value, and all four operations with negative numbers — the foundation for everything that follows.

4

Linear Equations

One-step, two-step, and multi-step equations; identities and contradictions; word problems.

5

Sets and Intervals

Set notation, subsets, union, intersection, complement, and interval notation — the precise language for describing solution sets.

6

Inequalities

Properties of inequalities, linear inequalities, compound inequalities, and absolute value equations and inequalities.

7

Polynomials

Adding, subtracting, multiplying, long division, synthetic division, and two full sections on factoring — the key to Chapters 10 and 11.

8

Graphing and Systems of Equations

The coordinate plane, slope, equations of lines, and solving systems by graphing, substitution, and elimination.

9

Functions and Variation

Relations and functions, function notation, domain, composition, inverse functions, and direct, inverse, and joint variation.

10

Quadratic Equations

Simplifying radicals, factoring, square roots, completing the square, the quadratic formula, and the discriminant — four methods, one clear strategy.

11

Rational and Radical Equations

Rational expressions and equations, radical equations, and equations quadratic in form — the culmination of everything in the book.
